BMW 340i M sport

The 2022 BMW 340i is equipped with a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 that develops 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The 340i's performance is augmented by a 48-volt mild hybrid system that supports faster and smoother engine performance while enhancing fuel efficiency
